Radar speed signs are effective in slowing cars down, particularly with today's highly visual, often distracted drivers. Also called speed display signs, driver feedback signs, and "your speed" signs, radar signs operate based on the feedback loop theory: when we are presented with information about our performance, we tend to notice and improve.
