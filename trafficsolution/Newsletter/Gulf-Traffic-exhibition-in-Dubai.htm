About Sinowatcher

 

 

Sinowatcher is the leading manufacturer of products and equipment that provide intelligent transportation systems and solutions. We specialize in design and development of intellegent transportation systems. Our products include  LED traffic Lights ,led traffic signal,traffic signal lights, intelligent traffic controllers, solar traffic lights, road studs, signs and radar speed system.

Gulf Traffic exhibition in Dubai

 

 


 

We are pleased to announce that Sinowatcher Technology will showcase its products at Gulf Traffic exhibition in Dubai, one of the most important ITS events of the year.


We will be located at the booth D03, where you can learn about our wide range of traffic lights and other ITS products as well as about latest development. Sinowatcher Technology team will be ready to help you find ideal traffic solution for your needs, and answer your questions there.


Gulf Region is developing at enormous speed as it is to invest US$121.3 billion in road and bridge infrastructure projects. For instance, Dubai’s Road and Transport Authority have approved a budget totaling $1.9 billion for 2014, and Saudi Arabia informed to upgrade the country’s transport infrastructure.


We strive to be part of this immense growth by providing intelligent transportation products, which will ensure road safety and steady traffic flow.
