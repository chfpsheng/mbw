High power LED Traffic Light ------★Energy-saving ★Elegant ★Stable ★Durable ★Economic

According to our customer’s need, Sinowatcher’s professional R&D team successfully optimizes high power LED traffic lights with advanced technology. It has become our hot product as it is very popular in Malaysian and European market and attracts many customers’ interests.
IP level 65, upgrade power supply with better moisture-proof electronic opponents to replace old ones, improve the ability of anticorrosion and anti-humidity even in rainy-belt. Besides, the working voltage is enlarged to AC85-265V, and the peek voltage can reach 290VAC.

Optimizing the circuit of power supply PCB board and LED PCB board, and keeping the metal cooling fin installed in the housing, we improve our product to be more stable.
The consumption of each 300mm high power light module is within 13W, which is lower than traditional ones of 15W, thus saving energy. Two-layers colored lens with top technology to provide optimal light distribution and elegant appearance. Over 8000 hours’ life span and 3 years’ quality assurance will earn your trust.
