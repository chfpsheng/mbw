Triggered Countdown Timer --- Shocked Innovation

Sinowatcher always keeps a close eye on market movement and continually issuing new design and customized products / solutions, which is the source of company vitality and improvements.

This month, we would like to introduce our Triggered Countdown Timer to you.

 

     Learning Type

> Works with fix time controller.
> Traffic schedule fixed.
> If schedule change, need 1-3 circles to learn again.

     Communicative Type

> Works with intelligent communicative controller.
> Schedule changes depending on practical traffic situation.
> Counting change immediately based on protocol communication
> Software inside countdown is authored based on controller protocol
> Seriously restricted by the confidentiality of protocol.

     Triggered Countdown Timer

It works with adaptive controller, which is able to send trigger signal to countdown timer with 200 msed flash turn-off time before the fixed last 00-99 seconds (part of the whole time). It’s featured with:

> Works with adaptive controller
> Triggered signal sent from controller to timer by 200 msed flash turn-off time
> No need learning circles after controller schedule change
> Counting the fixed last few seconds. ( traditional / sinowatcher 00-99)
> If trigger signal sending time change, only 1 circle needed for learning.
